Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member?

Membership is open to anyone who lives, works, or attends school in our service area. Simply visit any branch with a valid ID and make a minimum deposit of $25 to open your membership share account.

What documents do I need to open an account?

You'll need a valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, or state ID) and your Social Security number. For business accounts, additional documentation may be required.

How do I access online banking?

You can enroll in online banking at any branch or by calling our member services line. Once enrolled, visit our website or download our mobile app to access your accounts 24/7.

What are your loan rates?

Our rates are competitive and vary based on loan type, term, and creditworthiness. Visit our Services page for current rates or contact us for a personalized quote.

Do you offer notary services?

Yes, we offer free notary services to our members at all branch locations during business hours. Please call ahead to ensure a notary is available.

How do I report a lost or stolen card?

Contact us immediately at (555) 123-4567 or call our 24/7 card services line at (555) 123-CARD. You can also report it through our mobile app or online banking.
